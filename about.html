About Us

Oak & Flame is a family-run distillery nestled in the mountains of northern Greece. Drawing on generations of Greek tradition, we fuse ancient distillation methods with a passion for modern craft.

Our team of 10 artisans handcrafts small-batch whiskey from local barley and pure spring water, aging each drop in custom charred oak barrels. Every bottle reflects patience, fire, and true Greek spirit.

Our mission: share the heart of Greece with the world – one sip at a time.
